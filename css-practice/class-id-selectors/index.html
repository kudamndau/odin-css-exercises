Just as a disclaimer, I am not trying to make you believe me or what I say. I am just writing code and sharing my story. If you end up believing me, well kudos to me for being a very great and convincing storyteller.

Ummm... Where was I? The beginning? of what? My story? which one? Oh yeah my coding journey, my bad hehehe. Well my journey started last year, 2023 in October. My partner and I had been working with a guy who was a friend at that time but no longer is (story for another day, don't ask me about it right now). When he finished with his work I inspected the work thoroughly and unfortunately the perfectionist in me wasn't satisfied. I looked at the website and thought to myself "nah this ain't good at all!" It did't take me that long to make it known to my business counterparts. But as if that wasn't enough, the toxic trait in me just wouldn't let me end it at that. I just had to declare that I could do better than the web developer, imagine! I actually told everyone that I was going to make a better website than that, one that could fully represent our brand well. And that's how this journey to FAANGM-dom started.

So eversince then I have been learning on and off as a self-taught student, with the help of ChatGPT until recently when I enrolled into formal programmes, one of which is The Odin Project.
